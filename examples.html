Podcasts on Technology and the Future

General Tech, Business & The Future

  • a16z Podcast: From the venture capital firm Andreessen Horowitz, this podcast covers tech trends, culture, and the future, often featuring discussions on AI, crypto, and new business models.
  • Pivot: Hosted by Kara Swisher and Scott Galloway, this show offers sharp, unfiltered insights into the biggest stories in tech, business, and media.
  • What Next: TBD: A weekly podcast from Slate that explores the future of technology, power, and society, tackling complex topics with clear-eyed analysis.
  • Tech Talks Daily: A show that features interviews with leaders and innovators from around the world, discussing how emerging technologies like AI, IoT, and cybersecurity are transforming industries.
  • HBR IdeaCast: From Harvard Business Review, this weekly podcast features leading thinkers in business and management, often discussing innovation, technology strategy, and the future of work.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

  • The NVIDIA AI Podcast: This show features interviews with experts on how AI is being applied to solve complex problems in industries from healthcare to robotics.
  • Practical AI: A podcast that makes artificial intelligence practical, accessible, and approachable for everyone, covering the latest in machine learning and data science.
  • Google DeepMind: The Podcast: An award-winning series that goes behind the scenes at one of the world's leading AI labs to explore breakthroughs and their potential impact on the world.
  • Me, Myself, and AI: From MIT Sloan Management Review and BCG, this podcast explores how business leaders are implementing AI and how the technology is changing the way companies operate.
  • Lex Fridman Podcast: While covering a vast range of topics (including science, history, and philosophy), this podcast is renowned for its in-depth, long-form interviews with the world's top AI researchers and thinkers.

Cybersecurity

  • Darknet Diaries: An investigative podcast that features true stories from the dark side of the internet, including hacking, data breaches, and cybercrime.
  • Security Now: A long-running, in-depth weekly show that breaks down the latest cybersecurity news, threats, and vulnerabilities.
  • Risky Business: A popular weekly podcast that provides a lighthearted-but-thorough look at the business of information security, featuring news and interviews with industry experts.
  • CyberWire Daily: A concise, daily news briefing that gets you up to speed on the most important cybersecurity stories happening in government and the private sector.

Blockchain & Cryptocurrency

  • Unchained: Hosted by crypto-journalist Laura Shin, this podcast features in-depth interviews with the pioneers and key players in the blockchain and crypto space.
  • The Blockcrunch Podcast: A research-focused show that breaks down complex crypto projects and market trends, aimed at investors and builders.
  • Crypto 101: A great starting point for beginners, this podcast breaks down the fundamentals of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology in an easy-to-understand way.
  • The Pomp Podcast: Host Anthony "Pomp" Pompliano interviews prominent figures in finance and tech, with a heavy focus on the case for Bitcoin and the growth of the digital asset industry.

Robotics & Automation

  • The Robot Report Podcast: A weekly show for the robotics industry and academic community, featuring interviews with leading roboticists and analysis of the latest robotics news.
  • Soft Robotics Podcast: This podcast interviews researchers and experts from around the world to discuss the future of flexible, bio-inspired, and soft-bodied robots.
  • The Robot Industry Podcast: A show focused on the business of automation, covering the applications, challenges, and opportunities for robotics in manufacturing, logistics, and other sectors.
  • In Machines We Trust: A podcast from MIT Technology Review about the rise of AI and automation, exploring the impact these technologies are having on our lives and work.
